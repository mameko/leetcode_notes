**Greedy algorithms （来自leetcode）**

Greedy problems usually look like "Find minimum number of _something_ to do _something_" or "Find maximum number of _something_ to fit in _some conditions_", and typically propose an unsorted input.

> The idea of greedy algorithm is to pick the _locally_ optimal move at each step, that will lead to the _globally_ optimal solution.
